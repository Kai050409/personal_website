Biography

Kaining Zhang (b. Haicheng, Liaoning, China) is a photographic artist currently based in Melbourne, Australia.

Focusing primarily on medium and large format film processes, his work frequently returns to the concept of the Northeast Landscape. He explores themes of family lineage, collective memory, and the passage of time, most notably through his ongoing long-term visual project "So It Goes..."
